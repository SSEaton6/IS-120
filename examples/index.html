Examples of Stolen or Sampled Music

Because there is so much music out there, there are countless examples of possible cases of sampling and borrowing. Here we have list some of the more obvious and popular examples.

"All Summer Long" Samples

This one is interesting. This sample is legal, and Kid Rock actually credits Bob Seger, Lynyrd Skynyrd and Warren Zevon. The Wikipedia article about Kid Rock's version says this:""All Summer Long" is a song by American recording artist Kid Rock. It was released in 2008 as the third single from his seventh studio album, Rock n Roll Jesus. It was inspired by Bob Seger's song "Night Moves", and also samples "Sweet Home Alabama" by Lynyrd Skynyrd and "Werewolves of London" by Warren Zevon."
