About This Website

Interest

I decided to make this website because of my love for music. I listen to all kinds of music, and love listening for overlaps and commonalities between songs. I have always been interested in the legal side of it as well. I like to know the stories behind music, what songs inspired others, and how other artists incorporate these into their music. This website compares music that has been copied, sampled, and parodied with it's counterparts. I also discuss the legal side, if I know about it.
